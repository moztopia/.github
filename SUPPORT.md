There is **no formal support system**.

## 📌 Notes

- Internal contributors must reach out directly for guidance.  
- External contributors (public repos only) may open an **Issue** or **Pull Request**, but resolution still depends on James or Bruce.  
- Sensitive or security‑related concerns should follow the process in `SECURITY.md`.
